Gensync

An AI-powered platform that brings seniors and young learners together for real conversation and shared learning.

Data from the World Health Organization shows that around 25% of older adults globally face social isolation. A 2024 national survey also found that 33% of older adults often feel lonely.

This shows that many elderly people lack companionship and opportunities to be heard. At the same time, we observe that modern youth often use trendy internet slang, which older people struggle to understand. On the other hand, the elderly tend to speak in a detailed manner that younger people find hard to follow.

→ View Code

Our Match page uses a smart algorithm inspired by platforms like Tinder to help connect users across generations. When a young user signs up and indicates an interest—such as playing Go—we recommend older users who share the same passion. The same goes for seniors: if they've expressed a hobby or preference, the system will introduce them to like-minded young people. It's more than just matching—it's about sparking conversations and building friendships based on shared interests.

Our platform features a powerful chat function that integrates GPT-4o as a real-time "translator." Designed to bridge the communication gap between generations, this tool enables seamless conversation between young people and seniors. When users type in their native or preferred language, GPT-4o translates the messages in real time, helping both sides understand each other's tone, slang, and context more clearly. It's not just a translator—it's a bridge for empathy, connection, and meaningful dialogue.
